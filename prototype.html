Upload epilepsy imaging data to Pennsieve

This tool helps you organize your patient imaging data into the BIDS standard, validate it against the governance framework, and upload it to your site's Pennsieve workspace.

Before you start, confirm:
✓ DICOM and CT scans have been converted to NIfTI (.nii.gz)
✓ Structural MRI has been defaced / de-identified
✓ Filenames do not contain PHI (names, DOBs, MRNs)
✓ Your data covers the 3 clinical sessions: pre-implant, post-implant, post-surgery
✓ You have your Pennsieve API credentials ready
